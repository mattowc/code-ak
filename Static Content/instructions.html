THE ACUKNEE TREATMENT (the AcuKnee™ is set up for the right knee)
Please read PainWarrior® with AcuKneeTM user’s manual carefully and in full before you start using the device, giving heed the Warnings, Safety and Indications sections in the manual so you may achieve both confidence in understanding what you are doing and the full benefit of usage. # Instructions
1 Check the polarity of the electrodes in the AcuKnee™ (see the diagram on the reverse side).
2 Be sure the lead wires are securely connected, then apply the AcuKnee™ wrap to the knee.
3 Insert lead wires into Channel A and Channel B of the PainWarrior®
4 Insert a 9 Volt alkaline battery into the battery compartment and turn on the PainWarrior® by pressing the ON button (please note: electrodes must be in position before activation).
5 Using the PRG button select the COM program (default).
6 Slowly adjust left and right mA current output with the positive button. This starts the high frequency program and you will begin to feel the stimulation. Increase this to tolerance.
7 The PainWarrior® is equipped with a mA LOCKOUT. After one minute the stimulator will lock. To make any adjustment, you must press the negative button first to increase the mA.
8 Slowly adjust the left and the right current by selecting + or - buttons (both sides) to increase or decrease the current to the knee. This is a low frequency program and you may notice your muscle gently contracting. This is normal and harmless. The current should be increased to tolerance and should feel approximately equal on both the left and right side (numbers may be different.)
9 After fifteen minutes the program will automatically change to low frequency and you should readjust the mA frequency to tolerance.
10 You may notice some muscle contraction and this is normal.
11 After an additional fifteen minutes the PainWarrior® will turn itself off. You can track treatment time in the upper right hand corner of the PainWarrior® screen.
12 At the end of the program duration remove the wrap and replace the plastic electrode covers.
13 You can treat every day but no more than twice a day.

PainWarrior® with AcuKnee™

Before every use match the polarity shown in the images above for your knee. The AcuKnee™ is pre-set for the right knee. To treat the left knee reverse the polarity. To confirm the correct polarity, the outside top electrode (outside thigh) should have a black connector (Channel A) and the inside top (inside thigh) should have the red connector (Channel B).

AcuKnee Tips

  • Drop of gel each pad before use (last longer and better connectivity)
  • Replace protective sheet on pad after each use
  • Wrap snug – not tight
  • Turn on – if not on COM mode, select program until it cycles through to COM
  • Turn up + button on each side – should be equal and NOT painful (want to be aware of sensation, but not in pain).
  • After 1 minute on a setting, it has safety lock. If you want to increase level, you will need to select the (-) button first before increasing (+)
  • At 15 minutes it will automatically change to other frequency. You may need to adjust +/- settings to equal, aware, not painful
  • When done treating take off gently (don’t pull hard on pads – they will last longer)
  • Treat only once per day (more is not better)
  • Do not use ice on the treatment knee (it decreases effectiveness of treatment)

It has a cumulative effect. It will get better, last longer with each treatment.
